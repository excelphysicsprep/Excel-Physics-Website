About Excel Physics

Welcome to Excel Physics! Enhance your conceptual understanding of physics with the best!

Hello everyone, welcome to Excel Prep! We are a tutoring service founded by 3 high school juniors looking to help students learn physics in a clear and simple way. Physics is commonly viewed as one of the most difficult courses offered in high school, but with a streamlined approach focused on conceptual understanding, we believe that we can prepare students for this course. Unlike professional tutors/tutoring companies, our company is up-to-date and experienced with AP curriculum concepts taught in schools, having taken physics this year. The concepts we focus on parallel emphasized concepts at school.
